Capture-The-Flag to Chocolate-The-Flag About Our Team

Wargames Malaysia is a cybersecurity community that has been organizing annual Capture The Flag (CTF) events for years. Our team of skilled cybersecurity experts has created some of the most challenging and exciting CTF competitions, and our events have garnered a reputation for being both fun and educational. We take great pride in our ability to create CTF challenges that challenge even the most experienced cybersecurity professionals, while also providing opportunities for newcomers to learn and grow.

In addition to our CTF events, Wargames Malaysia is now excited to announce our new venture into the world of fine chocolate with Chocolate-The-Flag. We are committed to delivering the same level of excellence in our chocolates that we bring to our CTF events. With our expertise in both cybersecurity and chocolate making, we are confident that Chocolate-The-Flag will be a delicious success, capturing great minds and taste buds alike.
